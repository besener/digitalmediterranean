About Digital Mediterranean Project

This assignment introduces students to ArcGIS StoryMaps, a web-based story-authoring application. They will conduct a digital scholarly investigation and embark on a virtual journey through the art and architecture of the medieval Mediterranean world. They will create and share a vibrant digital map filled with monuments and objects accompanied by narrative and analytical text, images, sources, videos, audio, and other multimedia content.
