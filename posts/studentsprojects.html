September 1, 2025

Undergraduate students enrolled in ART_HIST 329: Art and Architecture of the Mediterranean World complete a final project using ArcGIS StoryMaps to visualize artistic exchange and cultural interactions in the medieval Mediterranean.

The final project helps students develop ArcGIS StoryMaps skills, applying an art historical perspective to visualize connections and movement in the medieval Mediterranean. They use narrative, visual analysis, research, and multimedia to present key monuments and objects.
